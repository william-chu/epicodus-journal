Why am I writing this?

To Reflect

Creating content for this journal forces me to sit down and think about what we learned each week during the program. Sifting through the material to point out key concepts reinforces my learning.

To Practice

Building this site gives me a self-directed opportunity to apply knowledge from the classroom. I can experiment and learn by actively coding a personal project.

To Share

Communicating allows us to understand different approaches people take to solve the same problem. My goal is to create content that is useful to others that are enrolled at or considering Epicodus.
