Epicodus Journal

- Chronicling My Coding Bootcamp Experience -

Why am I writing this?

To Reflect

Creating content for this journal forces me to sit down and think about what we learned each week during the program. Sifting through the material to point out key concepts reinforces my learning.

To Practice

Building this site gives me a self-directed opportunity to apply knowledge from the classroom. I can experiment and learn by actively coding a personal project.

To Share

Discussing our knowledge allows us to see and understand different ways to solve the same problem. My goal is to create content that is useful to others that are enrolled at or considering Epicodus.

Navigating the site:

The journal is organized into sections covering each of the 5 week increments that makes up the learning track at Epicodus. Within each section, there will be a further breakdown into the individual weeks. Information will be broken apart based on the subject material and will be identified using these categories:

Command Line

Overview of Bash and Git commands

Atom

Tips and shortcuts for configuring and using the Atom editor

Good Habits

Suggestions for prep that you can do outside of the classroom to set yourself up for success

HTML/CSS

Takeaways for HTML and CSS

JavaScript/jQuery

Takeaways for JavaScript and jQuery

Overlap

Connect the dots between concepts for overall understanding
